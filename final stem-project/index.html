Project Goals, Objectives, and Impact

Primary Goal

The primary goal of the STEM Faculty Evaluation Automation System is to modernize and standardize the faculty evaluation process at Prince George's Community College (PGCC). This involves transitioning from manual, paper-based methods to a streamlined, digital system that ensures consistency, efficiency, and transparency.

Specific Objectives

Reduce Processing Time

Automate key steps in the evaluation process to significantly reduce the time required for completion.

Enhance Consistency

Implement standardized criteria and procedures to ensure fairness and uniformity across all faculty evaluations.

Deliver Timely Feedback

Provide faculty with timely and constructive feedback to support their professional growth and development.

Improve Transparency

Create a transparent evaluation process where faculty can easily access their evaluation status, feedback, and relevant documentation.

Broader Impact

Improve Education Quality

By improving faculty evaluations, the system will contribute to enhancing the quality of education provided to students.

Streamline Administration

The automated system will streamline administrative tasks, freeing up staff to focus on other important activities.

Support Faculty Development

The system will support faculty development by providing clear feedback and identifying areas for improvement.

Increase Overall Productivity

By automating and streamlining processes, the system will increase overall productivity within the STEM faculty.
